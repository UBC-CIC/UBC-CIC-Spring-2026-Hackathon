# AWS Jam FAQ

### When will AWS Jam be held?
Monday, May 4 2026, starting at 11:00am. An introduction to AWS Jam session will be held for 30 minutes, and will take place via Zoom.
### How will I get access?
Access details will be shared during the introduction at the start of the Zoom session and posted in Discord. Please make sure you are signed up AWS Jam using your UBC email address.
### Will I be with my team for AWS Jam?
This is an individual Jam session. Each participant works on their own, with a team of one. This ensures everyone gains direct experience with the same challenges and AWS services.
### How much time will I have?
You will have 4 hours of support via Discord to complete the challenges, beginning at 11:00am on Monday, May 4.
### How many challenges are there, and what types can I expect?
You will work through a range of challenges, including Generative AI, application development and DevOps, and security. Challenges vary in difficulty from easy to medium and hard.
### Are there prizes?
There are no prizes for the AWS Jam. Participants will be recognized on a leaderboard. Prizes will be awarded during the hackathon instead.
